Northern Ontario Mine Site PPE Requirements (Reference Summary)

- Hard hats are mandatory in all active work zones, processing areas, and anywhere overhead hazards exist.
- High-visibility vests or clothing are required where mobile equipment, haul trucks, or pedestrian traffic overlap.
- Supervisors must verify PPE before authorizing floor entry at shift start.
- If PPE cannot be confirmed due to image angle or occlusion, treat as "unclear" and require a physical re-check — do not assume compliance.
- Fatigue screening is a visual aid only; moderate signals warrant closer supervision, not automatic removal from duty without supervisor assessment.
