OSHA General PPE Guidance (Reference Summary)

- Employers must ensure appropriate PPE is used when workplace hazards cannot be eliminated through engineering or administrative controls.
- Head protection is required where there is potential for injury from falling objects or fixed hazards.
- High-visibility apparel improves worker conspicuity in environments with vehicle movement.
- Document safety observations and corrective actions for audit purposes.
- Missing critical PPE near operating machinery should trigger immediate stop-work until corrected.
