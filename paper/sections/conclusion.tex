\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented Northern Shift Guard, a zone-aware vision-language framework for explainable and auditable mining shift-start safety screening. Our three-layer architecture---vision evidence, zone compliance, and reasoning---produces supervisor actions grounded in regulatory context with full audit trails. The zone-flip experiment demonstrates that context-aware compliance reduces false alarms compared to global-rule baselines while maintaining safety in high-risk zones.

Northern Shift Guard is not ``helmet yes/no.'' It is: what was seen $\rightarrow$ what this zone requires $\rightarrow$ what to do now $\rightarrow$ why---with a record.
