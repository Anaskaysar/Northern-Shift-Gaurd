100-WORD ABSTRACT (92 words)
================================================================================

Shift-start PPE checks in mining are often manual, inconsistent, and undocumented. Global computer vision systems flag missing hard hats even when workers enter surface areas where helmets are not required under zone-specific regulations. Northern Shift Guard is a three-layer vision-language framework for explainable, auditable shift-start screening. Structured vision evidence is checked against zone-specific PPE requirements, then reasoned into prioritized supervisor actions with full audit records. The same worker photo can produce different compliance outcomes for surface yard versus active stope zones. We present system architecture, evaluation design, and results from a deployed prototype.
